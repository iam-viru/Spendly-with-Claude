Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your email address and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as login times, used solely for security and debugging.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what is needed to provide the Service.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate Spendly for you:

  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To display and manage your expense records.
  • To generate summaries and breakdowns within your dashboard.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the Service.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before being saved. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. We may use third-party services for infrastructure (such as hosting), but these providers are contractually required to keep your data confidential and may not use it for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided on our website. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
